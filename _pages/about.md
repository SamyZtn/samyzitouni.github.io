I am a second year PhD candidate at [PSE](https://www.parisschoolofeconomics.eu/en/) and [EDF R&D Paris Saclay](edf.fr). I am supervised by [Carine Staropoli](https://www.parisschoolofeconomics.eu/equipes/carine-staropoli/) (PSE), Corinne Chaton and Valérie Lesgards (EDF).

I work on the French energy renovation policy for households. Especially, I seek to understand how the current policy instrument allow public powers to draw from the energy savings potential. I also test innovative subsidies for energy renovation.
